What is Graphic Designing?

Graphic designing is the art and practice of creating visual content, including images, typography, and layout, to convey information or messages to a specific audience. Graphic designers use various tools and software to create designs that can be used for a wide range of purposes, such as advertising, branding, marketing, and communication.
